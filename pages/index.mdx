# Introduction

Qbox is a framework. This framework strives to be greater and combines with [overextended's resources](https://github.com/overextended) to achieve this greatness. Come witness the greatness and be overwhelmed by it!


## Contributing

Contributions are always welcome, but we prefer quality over quantity! Please read our [contributing guidelines](https://qbox-project.github.io/contributors) to learn how best to contribute.


## Support

Support for Qbox is provided for by the community in the support channels of the [Qbox Discord](https://discord.gg/qbox).

Luckily, Qbox benefits from a great community with varied experience and we encourage everyone to help eachother in a friendly and respectable manner.
